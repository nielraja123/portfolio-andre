Newest Project

Take a look at some of the projects I've created.

Cube Craze

Cube Craze is a PC-based 3D Tetris game that requires players to use strategy in arranging blocks to form horizontal lines quickly.

The more horizontal lines that are successfully created, the more points the player will earn. The longer the game duration, the faster the blocks will fall.

Nyaman

Nyaman is a website-based hotel booking application that aims to provide a convenient and easy experience for users in searching and booking hotel accommodation. The app offers a comprehensive and intuitive range of features.

Users can search for hotel rooms based on date of stay, number of guests, and other preferences. With a user-friendly interface, users can easily navigate the site, view detailed information about the hotel, including pictures, facilities, guest reviews, and available room rates.

WasteLess

WasteLess is a website-based waste bank application that is mainly aimed at helping households and industries with waste management issues. The four categories of waste that can be collected and traded in this app are plastic, glass & cans, electronics, and paper.

In this app, users can sell and buy waste at the same time. There are 3 main features in WasteLess; Marketplace (for buying and selling waste), Penjualan (for inputting products that users want to sell), and Pendapatan (for viewing income that users have earned).
